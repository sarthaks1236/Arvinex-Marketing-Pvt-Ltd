Website Development

Building Our Own Lead-Generation Website

The site you're on right now — designed to load fast, work on every device, and turn visitors into enquiries.

IndustryMarketing Agency
ServicesWebsite, SEO, UI Design
Timeline2 Weeks

The Challenge

As a marketing agency, our own website is the first thing potential clients judge us on. It needed to load quickly, work flawlessly on mobile (where most of our enquiries come from), and make it effortless for a visitor to reach out — without forcing them through a clunky multi-step contact process.

Our Approach

We built the site as a fast, lightweight static site — no heavy frameworks, no unnecessary page weight. Every section was designed to answer a specific visitor question (What do you do? Who have you worked with? What does it cost? How do I reach you?) in order, with a one-click WhatsApp button and click-to-call phone number always within reach.

What We Delivered

  • Fully responsive, mobile-first website with smooth animations
  • One-click WhatsApp and call buttons on every page
  • On-page SEO structure with schema markup for search visibility
  • A simple, non-intimidating quote request form

The Outcome

The result is a site that loads quickly even on slower mobile connections, clearly explains our services and pricing upfront, and removes as much friction as possible between a visitor deciding they're interested and actually reaching out — whether that's a form, a WhatsApp message, or a direct call.
