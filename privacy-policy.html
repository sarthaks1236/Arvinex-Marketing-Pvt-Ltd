Arvinex Marketing Agency Pvt Ltd ("Arvinex", "we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect through this website, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

We retain enquiry and project information for as long as necessary to provide our services and to comply with legal or accounting obligations, after which it is deleted or anonymised.

5. Your Rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by emailing us at arvinextradingco@gmail.com. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.
